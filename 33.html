Followers Race

 16% of internet users aged 16-64 use Twitter for brand research

Around the same number of people also use instant messaging and live chatbots to conduct research online. For marketers, this means that you need to keep your brand account up to date. Of course, not everyone needs to post five or six times a day, but if audiences are searching for brands online and scoping them out, it’ll help your credibility if your account is active and regularly posting valuable content.
