Business accounts post an average of 1.6 times a day

To break that down even further: for the average Instagram Business account, 62.7% of all main feed posts are photos, while 16.3% are videos and 21% are photo carousels.

Again, every brand is different, but it’s helpful to see the the competition is (on average!) mixing things up with the types of content it posts. If you’ve stuck steadfastly to a photos-only game plan, maybe now’s the time to start diversifying.
